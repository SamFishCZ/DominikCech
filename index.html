Greener Pastures

Platform:

Android OS

Project Duration:

3 Months

Personal Tools:

Unity, Reaper, Wwise

Team Size:

8

Playthrough:

Project Overview:

The latest project was an android farming experience designed as a part of my third year studies. Greener Pastures educates on the food cycle and through events and objectives teaches the player how farming impacts the world, what is neccessary to preserve it and where science might go to make it sustainable in the long-term for planet Earth.

Roles:

Economy Design

My main role in economy design was designing production values of building to reflect real world farming as well as keep a healthy balance for players to explore variations of building and determine the best combinations.

Gameplay Design

The key part of this project was based on the initial overall design of Greener Pastures. My main work was designing building types and their usage, upgrades and the grid size to allow for desired game time engagement.

Sound Design

My responsibility with sound design was sountrack composition and audio editing. In later stages of sound design implementation and packaging through Wwise for reaper was also a part of my contributions.

Cat Burglar

Platform:

Microsoft Windows

Project Duration:

3 Months

Personal Tools:

Unreal Engine 4

Team Size:

6

Playthrough:

Project Overview:

Cat Burlar is a top down action stealth game developed as a part of a team for a university project. The player takes control of a thief and while avoiding guards and defensive mechanism is required to steal as many cat treasures as possible and succesfully guide to the exit.

Roles:

Gameplay Design

My main role for gameplay design consisted of level interactables such as cameras, lasers or disguises as well as creating level goals and requirements such as keycards and guard patrol routes.

Animation Programming

Key aspect of this role was to create scripts and blueprints for character animations through Unreal Engine 4 and further create key blueprints for proper character functions in the project itself.

AI Design & Programming

AI created the key part of our game and as my it was my responsibility to create a functioning AI. The result was an AI with the possibility to patrol specific routes or randomly wander select areas of the level.
